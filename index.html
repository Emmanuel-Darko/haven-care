Our SERVICES

We offer the following services...

Click to expand...

We provide skilled nursing services such as, wound dressings, rehabilitation services, tube feedings, vital signs monitoring and recording, injections(intravenous) etc to enhance and promote rapid changing health status of our clients

We care for patients with diverse medical conditions in their home's comfort.
Includes: giving medications, personal hygiene, changing dressing, helping with walking aids, intake and output, housekeeping services for clients.

We undertake physio sessions with our clients most especially with physical injuries or illness of to regain range of movement and control pain

We help you develop, recover and improve as well as maintain the skills needed for their daily living and working activities eg.
a. Self care (brushing of teeth,clothe buttoning etc).
b. Fine motor skills (controlling a pencil, grasping etc).

Educational and counseling services, helps client to determine appropriate health care plan to promote health status for themselves and families, Organize for institutions Health talks, walks and seminars, health event organizing and promoting, health screenings programs etc.
